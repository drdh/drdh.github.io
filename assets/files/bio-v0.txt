Heng Dong​​ is a Ph.D. student at the ​Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS), Tsinghua University, advised by ​​Prof. Chongjie Zhang​​ and ​​Prof. Yi Wu​, under the leadership of Turing Award laureate ​​Prof. Andrew Yao​. His research aims to develop ​superhuman AI agents​ through learning from interactions (e.g., RL, robot control & design, multi-agent) and modern models (e.g., LLMs & VLMs, diffusion models, flow Models). He received his ​B.S. in Computer Science​ from the ​University of Science and Technology of China (USTC)​​ in 2020 and began his Ph.D. at Tsinghua University the same year.
